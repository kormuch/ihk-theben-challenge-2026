# EMC & Electrical Conformity Compliance Agent Skill

Scope: EMC, CE-related electrical evidence, ESD, surge, transients, robustness, and building automation requirements.

Inputs:

- Product family.
- Nominal voltage and electrical attributes.
- EMC or electrical test reports.
- Target market.
- Standards validity context.

Checks:

- Electrical attributes exist for relevant product families.
- EMC evidence is mapped to product family and target market.
- Directive version and placing-on-market date are visible.

Output: advisory finding with evidence gaps, standard references, risk severity, and recommended review actions.
