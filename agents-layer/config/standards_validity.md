# Standards Validity Model

Standards, directives, norms, draft rules, and market-specific requirements are time-bound. Agents must evaluate the product family, target market, lifecycle state, date of placing on the market, applicable version, current version, transition period, and grandfathering rules.

Rules:

- The date of placing the product on the market is mandatory for compliance reasoning where standard versions changed.
- Draft-only rules may be used for readiness advice but not as final compliance decisions.
- Superseded standards may remain relevant for products already placed on the market.
- Findings must cite the identifier and validity status used.
- Human review is required before any certification-impacting interpretation is accepted.
