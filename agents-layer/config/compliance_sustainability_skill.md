# Sustainability & Environment Compliance Agent Skill

Scope: ESPR, DPP sustainability evidence, carbon footprint, materials, circularity, recycling, repairability, and end-of-life requirements.

Inputs:

- Product family and lifecycle state.
- Target market.
- Date of placing on the market.
- Environmental declaration evidence.
- Materials and footprint attributes.
- Standards validity references.

Checks:

- Required environmental evidence exists.
- Sustainability fields are plausible and traceable.
- DPP sustainability fields have evidence references.
- Transition and placing-on-market date are considered.

Output: advisory finding with missing evidence, standard references, confidence, and recommended actions.
